Data analysis

As for the company's need to increase sales and change the source of customers, three fifths of the people choose the company's need to increase sales.
They think that only in this way can the company increase its income. Another part of the people think that the need to change the source of customers can not be limited to one region or only women.

For companies that need to innovate products and change their business models, most people choose to change their business models.
They need companies to increase their online procurement channels and have their own shopping carts to improve the company's development.
Others believe that we should innovate products to increase popularity and performance.

As for the company's need to increase staff training, most people think that it is necessary to strengthen staff training so that they can learn more, understand new things and improve their skills.
A small number of people think that there is no need to increase training, have enough confidence in their own technology, and do not waste too much company funds.
